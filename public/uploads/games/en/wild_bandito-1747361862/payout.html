  • Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatter symbol.
  • Gold Framed symbols appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only.
Gold Framed Symbols
  • During any spins, some symbols (excluding Wild symbol and Scatter symbol) in reels 2, 3 and/or 4 may appear in gold.
  • At every new round after the new symbols have cascaded down, any gold framed symbol(s) that is involved in a win in the previous round will be transformed into Wild symbol(s).
Multiplier
  • At the start of any main game spin, the win multiplier is x1.
  • During any spins, if there are one or more winning symbols on the reels, after wins are paid and the new symbols have cascaded down, the win multiplier will be increased by 1 .
Free Spins Feature
  • 3 Scatter symbols appearing anywhere will trigger the Free Spins Feature with 12 free spins. Each additional Scatter symbol will trigger 2 more free spins.
  • During any free spin, if there are one or more winning symbols on the reels, after wins are paid and the new symbols have cascaded down, the win multiplier will be increased by 1 .
  • Free spins can be retriggered.
1,024 Ways
  • Bet ways win if the winning symbols are in succession from the leftmost reel to the right.
  • Total number of winning bet ways for each symbol are calculated by multiplying the number of adjacent winning symbols on each symbols from the leftmost reel to the right.
